प्रज्ञानं ब्रह्म , अहं ब्रह्मास्मि,तत्त्वमसि,अयमात्मा ब्रह्म

"My thoughts" these words makes sense in the logic systems used in social rules of conversations. But in a different system these words are absurd as I am the thought and by saying my thoughts we are implying there are thoughts that i have that are mine in a possesive way. But since having something assumes existence of a thing that is separate from me that can be achieved and lost and since I am the thought so these words implies I can have myself which is absurd as I can never have myself since the two are not seperate from each other. But again all this conclusion is nonsensical in some other system. So the problem is much deeper then can be conveyed.

The invention of words and languages in conveying information was a crucial milestone in human evolution. It has helped humans achieve complex yet sustainable society and it has also helped us go so far in STEM field. But just like any other invention it only takes us so far. Trying to write about myself here will be in contradiction to what I am saying.

तत्त्वमसि

That word, on experience, resolves all the issues mentioned / unmentioned in any texts.
Anywhere I use the words reffering to me or my activities, including in these lines, is used just for social convenience. Thats not the way "I" experience "myself". The reason of achieving or doing anything, as mentioned in the home page, is to check the practicality of this self-less experience. These experiences experienced by the experiences, are part of an experiment by "me" on "myself".
