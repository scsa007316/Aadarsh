I am something of a Physicist, Philosopher, Polyglot Programmer, IT Technician, Mathematician myself.

I am a physics student by profession. My academics is mostly revolving around science and mathematics. By personnel curiosity, I learned about the technological advances taking place in the world and also learned about the philosophical dialogues that one encounters when one starts enquiring deeply about anything. Apart from western philosophy, I am also exposed to several eastern philosophies such as Non-dualism, Budhhism, Taoism etc. I also believe that technology will play major role in upcoming discoveries. AI is an example of such technology and it has the potential to help in settling the long age philosophical questions for once and all.
My thoughts are influenced by rational skeptics such as the four horseman, James Randy, Arthur Schopenhaur etc. Personally I like to keep an open minded attitude towards new ideas and theories expaining the empirical evidences. Sometimes I do like to question our best known facts and theories and like to contemplate on the alternatives.
